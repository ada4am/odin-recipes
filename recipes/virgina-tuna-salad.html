Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 (5 ounce)can tuna, drained & flaked
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 stalk celery, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Direction

  • Step 1

    • place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water, Bring water to a boil and immediatly remove from heat, Cover and let egg stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes, Remove from hot water, Cool for 5 minutes, peel and chop into bite-sized pieces

  • Step 2

    • In a medium bowl, mix together tuna and mayonnaise, Mix in egg, celery, relish and black pepper
